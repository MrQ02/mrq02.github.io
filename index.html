Why AI?

In 2007, a Japanese company created the concept of "Virtual Singer".
At 11, I suffered from depression because I couldn't accept "death" and "the end of the universe". Everything was meaningless to me because everything ends.
In 2010-2012, the "Virtual Singer" Hatsune Miku became popular (although it faded away). Some of her songs, composed by many young talents, inspired me to think about life and death at a deeper level. That is when my fear gradually transformed into my desire for discovering the meaning of existence.
Since primary school, I enjoy the sense of achievements through creating things. I created small robots with Lego NXT, VEX, and Arduino as a side hobby back then.

"If I love creating things so much and I want to know the truth behind existence so much, why don't I just combine them?"
"How?" "Create super-intelligence."
"Are you stupid? It is impossible!" "Yeh... you are right."
Scared of impossibility and uncertainty, I didn't take any action.
3 years later, Due to some fatal financial crisis in my family, my life was crashed by risks and uncertainties. Seeking a safe shelter, I gave up my dream and talents and followed the mediocre path of "graduate, get a high-wage job, work as a white collar, retire".
I kept making wrong decisions, and I couldn't find the "safe shelter" I was expecting. Gradually, I realized that the only safe shelter I could ever have throughout my life, is myself.
So I picked up my dream of creating super-intelligence, and I have been working on it since 2019/06. Surprisingly, with huge risks and uncertainties along the way, I never got tired of it. I found happiness in it.
Therefore, AI.

Why Stats?

It all originated from an accident.
One of my important family members unfortunately fell into a financial fraud (which he still unfortunately believes to be authentic because he doesn't understand statistics), and this has driven me crazy for 2 years already.
As I was struggling to find the loophole of this fraud, I accidentally encountered the concept "Survivorship Bias".
"Wait a minute, isn't this the Bayes Theorem I learned in Machine Learning???"
"WHAT? We can apply Bayes Theorem to our daily life just like that??? Wait a minute, is Statistics this powerful?! OMG Why didn't I know this before?"

The next day, this Youtube video randomly popped up on recommendations (stop taking my cookies, Google!). But it is this video that directly inspired me of my true passion.
As the combination of Mathematics and Social Sciences, Statistics can teach me the (cruel) reality behind this world and humanity more than any other subject, and I love that.
I want to learn about facts. I want to gain knowledge from the factual data. I want to know the truth!
I have been searching for my true passion since 2016, and it was this moment at which I felt the genuine joy and excitement from the bottom of my heart.
I felt, confident, and hopeful. Statistics perfectly synergizes with my dream, my talent, and my career options. For years I have been living in fear of risks and uncertainty because of financial struggles, Statistics offers me a path of hope.
Therefore, Stats.
